The prototype web site does not carry a minified version of prototype.js nor do they
correctly stamp the downloaded file with the prototype.js version number.

Here is what you do:
1. Download prototype.js from the official site (http://prototypejs.org/download/).
2. Give it the right name:
	mv prototype.js prototype-1.7.1.js
3. Compress it via yui-compressor:
	yui-compressor prototype-1.7.1.js > prototype-1.7.1.min.js
